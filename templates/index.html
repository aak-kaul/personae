How it works

1 · Preprocess — median imputation + standardization so no single high-magnitude field dominates distances.   2 · PCA — reduce redundancy and project to 2-D for visualization.   3 · K-means — partition into segments, k chosen with the elbow + silhouette.   4 · Personas — each segment's average profile becomes a named, plain-language persona grounded in real statistics.
